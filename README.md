📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance using Excel’s advanced features like Charts and Data Visualization tools. It helps identify top-performing regions, profitable product categories, and areas needing improvement.

---

🎯 Objectives

🔹Analyze regional sales and profit performance.

🔹Identify high and low-performing product categories.

🔹Monitor discount impact on revenue.

🔹Provide data-driven insights for decision-making.

---

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

🔹Microsoft Excel

🔹Data Cleaning & Transformation

🔹Conditional Formatting

🔹Dashboard Design & Visualization

---

📊 Key Features

📍 Region-wise Sales & Profit Analysis.

🌍 Interactive Map Visualization for Location Insights.

📈 Sales vs Profit Trend Analysis.

🧩 Product Sub-Category Breakdown.

📊 Category-wise Sales Distribution (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies).

🎯 KPI Metrics (Total Sales, Profit, Discounted Price).

---

📈 Key Insights

🔹Certain regions like Ontario and West show higher sales and profit contribution.

🔹Discounts significantly impact overall profitability.

🔹Product categories such as Technology contribute majorly to revenue.

🔹Some sub-categories generate high sales but lower profit margins.

---

✅ Results

🔹Improved visibility into business performance.

🔹Identified profitable regions and categories.

🔹Enabled better strategic decision-making using data insights.
